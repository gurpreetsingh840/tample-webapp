Khalsa School

Our Mission

To educate children in Sikhi, Punjabi language, and cultural heritage while fostering leadership qualities and promoting Gurmat values in a safe, nurturing environment that encourages learning through understanding and practice.

Schedule

  • Sundays 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Assembly & Attendance: 10:55 AM
  • Class Time: 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Assembly & Announcements: 12:45 PM

Grade Levels

  • Pre-K & Kindergarten (Ages 4-5)
  • Level 1 (Ages 6-7)
  • Level 2 (Ages 8-9)
  • Level 3 (Ages 10-11)
  • Level 4 (Ages 12-13)
  • Youth Group (Ages 14+)

Curriculum

  • Gurmukhi Reading & Writing
  • Sikh History & Values
  • Kirtan & Tabla Classes
  • Nitnem & Path Reading
  • Gurmat Knowledge
  • Cultural Activities
