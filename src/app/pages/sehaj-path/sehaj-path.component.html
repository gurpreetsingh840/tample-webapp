Sehaj Path

"Sehaj Path offers a flexible and personal way to connect with Guru Granth Sahib Ji's teachings, allowing devotees to immerse themselves in the divine words at their own pace."

Sehaj Path is a recitation of Guru Granth Sahib from beginning to end, but unlike Akhand Path, it doesn't have to be continuous. A person or group of persons can read the holy text according to their schedule and complete the reading of Guru Granth Sahib at their own pace.

The Sehaj Path is a flexible and convenient way to engage with the teachings of Guru Granth Sahib. It allows individuals or groups to read the holy text at their own pace, making it accessible to those with busy schedules or other commitments.

The Sehaj Path can be performed in various settings, including Gurudwaras, homes, or other suitable locations. It is a time for reflection, meditation, and spiritual growth, providing an opportunity to connect with the divine teachings of the Guru.

The Sehaj Path is often performed to commemorate important events, seek blessings, or provide spiritual support during times of hardship. It is a cherished tradition in Sikhism that brings peace, tranquility, and divine blessings to the participants.
