Sehaj Path

Sehaj Path is a complete reading of the Guru Granth Sahib, the Sikh Holy Scriptures, at a slower pace. It allows individuals to connect deeply with the teachings and is often done over a period of time.

The Sehaj Path is a more deliberate and paced reading of the Guru Granth Sahib, allowing for a deeper connection and understanding of the Guru's teachings. It is often performed by individuals or families over a period of days, weeks, or even months.

The Sehaj Path is a personal and spiritual journey that allows the reader to immerse themselves in the divine wisdom and guidance of the Guru Granth Sahib. It is a time for reflection, meditation, and spiritual growth.

The Sehaj Path can be performed at home, in a Gurdwara, or any other suitable location. It is a flexible and accessible practice that can be adapted to fit the reader's schedule and lifestyle.

The Sehaj Path is a beautiful and meaningful way to connect with the Guru's teachings and to experience the peace and tranquility that comes from immersing oneself in the divine word.
