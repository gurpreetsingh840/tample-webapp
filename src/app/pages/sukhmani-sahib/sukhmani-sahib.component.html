Sukhmani Sahib

Sukhmani Sahib is a set of hymns in the Guru Granth Sahib, the Sikh Holy Scriptures, that provide peace and comfort to the mind. It is often recited to seek solace and spiritual upliftment.

Sukhmani Sahib is a set of hymns divided into 24 sections which appear in the Guru Granth Sahib, the Sikh Holy Scriptures. The word Sukhmani literally means Peace in your mind.

The recitation of Sukhmani Sahib is believed to bring peace, tranquility, and spiritual solace to the reader and the listener. It is often recited during times of stress, anxiety, or hardship to seek comfort and divine support.

Sukhmani Sahib is a powerful and uplifting prayer that can be recited individually or in a group. It is a beautiful expression of faith and devotion that brings the reader closer to the divine presence of the Guru.

The recitation of Sukhmani Sahib is a cherished tradition in Sikhism and is often performed in Gurdwaras, homes, and other suitable locations. It is a time for reflection, meditation, and spiritual growth.
